Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the Rafter Laughter website is to advertise the Rafter Laughter tourism and rental business.

As a locally owned rafting guide and rental shop we aim to bring smiles to the faces of our customers as we line our pockets with their cash.

This site will provide information regarding our hours, prices, policies and other business related activities. Customers will also be able to fill out waivers from the site.

We'd also like to website to be a place for customers to share reviews and memories of their rafting adventures.

Audience

Our audience will be a variety of individuals from all walks of life. From families looking to have a nice weekend together to thrill seekers and college students.

Anyone looking to have an exciting time on the river or a nice relaxing float is welcome.

We expect that our site will be accessed from variety of platforms, mobile devices, pc's etc.

In order to meet the needs of those visiting the site we will make sure the interface is accesible to everyone, with information readily available.
