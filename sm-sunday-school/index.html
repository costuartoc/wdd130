Our Purpose

This site is for members of the SM YSA ward and visitors to have access to information regarding our current topic of study, information about our SS goals, links to Church study resources, links to SMYSA social media websites and calendar, and SS contact information in addition to missionary contact information. Members will want to have this site bookmarked for anything related to SMYSA. They will also learn about the SM SS through the information provided. Visitors will want to use the links provided to have personal study, share insights on the SMYSA social media account and to contact members of the YSA SS or the missionaries with questions they have.
